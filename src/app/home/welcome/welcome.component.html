How it Works

1. Do a Booking

Select the right booking and post your requirement. The application will return a booking id to track the booking status. Click on the SUBSCRIBE button to the booking notification.

2. Approve the Booking

The booking is approved by rinteger team after evaluating your booking with in 24 hours

3. Execute the Booking

The booking is processed and communicated to the customer using push notification. The booking id us used to track the different stages of the booking

4. Deliver the Booking

Booking completion follows entire booking cycle and the feedback is collected to completed the delivery.
